    \chapter*{Abstract}
    Thesis is dedicated to the design and implementation of a compiler for languages generated by $LR(1)$ grammars. We focus our attention on the $C0$ language, which combines syntax of $C$ and semantics of $Pascal$. The ambiguity of the language is evaluated, after which its classification with respect to $LR(1)$ grammars is determined, culminating in the construction of a comprehensive compiler built in $Java$.
